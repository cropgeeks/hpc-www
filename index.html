CPUs

Fifty-five physical compute nodes offer access to 112 Intel CPUs providing 1,764 compute cores (3,528 threads) in total, broken down as 100 2nd-Gen Xeon Scalable (Cascade Lake; 2019) processors, six 1st-Gen Xeon Scalable (Skylake; 2019) processors and six Xeon E5-v4 (Broadwell; 2017) processors.

Memory

The cluster has 15,296 GB of available DDR4 memory. Two large memory machines provide 1.5 TB and 3.0 TB, alongside forty-eight nodes with 192 GB of memory, two nodes with 256 GB of memory, and two nodes with 384 GB of memory.

Storage

An eight-node BeeGFS parallel storage system (running over a 25-gigabit network backbone) provides access to 1.5 petabytes of storage. This is complemented by a addtional petabyte of backup capacity.
